Slide 5

Challenges

  • Landsat data quality in the past is not optimal.
  • Training data only exists in recent years.
  • DL models trained with Landsat in recent years, cannot transfer to the past.

Slide 6

Solution

  • We developed a novel method.
  • We simulated the quality of the "80s" Landsat to the recent Landsat data.
  • By doing this, we can introduce the data variations to the DL models.

Slide 7

Model training

  • Our training data is available in 3 periods (2015, 2018, 2021).
  • We simulated the training data to previous year.
